Life expectancy by sex

  • In every country the life expectancy of women is higher than the life expectancy of men as this chart shows.
  • If we draw a diagonal parity line, we will observe that all countries are positioned above this line, indicating that in every country, a newborn girl can expect to live longer than a newborn boy.
  • Interestingly, this chart shows that while the female advantage exists everywhere, the cross-country differences are large. In Russia women live 10 years longer than men; in Bhutan the difference is less than half a year.
  • The vertical axis shows the life expectancy of men. The horizontal axis shows the life expectancy of women
